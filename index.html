What is This Workshop?

Why make your own font?
  • Your text looks personal and unique
  • It matches your handmade work
  • You can edit and resize text easily
  • It looks professional

TASK 1: Upload and Clean Your Font

Upload your scan and clean each letter.

Steps to Follow

1
Upload Your Scan
  • Log in to Calligraphr
  • Go to "My Fonts"
  • Click "Upload Template"
  • Choose your JPEG file
2
Check Your Letters
  • Look at each letter
  • Check they are in the centre
  • Look for missing letters
3
Clean Each Letter
  • Click on a letter
  • Click "Edit Character"
  • Delete any dirt or marks
  • Do this for ALL letters
Important: Clean every letter! Small marks will show every time you use that letter.
4
Build Your Font
  • Give your font a name
  • Click "Build"
  • Check how it looks
5
Download and Install
  • Download the font file
  • Mac: Double-click file ‚Üí "Install Font"
  • Windows: Right-click ‚Üí "Install"
Important: Close and reopen Photoshop to see your new font!
